Tarnowskie Gory and Erlangen

The county Erlangen-Höchstadt have been partner-county of polish county Tarnowskie Góry since 2005. The county Tarnowskie Góry is located in upper part of Upper Silesian Industrial District and it consist of nine gmina and four of them have town charter. Tarnowskie Góry shares border with Będzin, Lubliniec and Gliwice. Total population of county Tarnowskie Góry is 143 thousands people and the county covers an area of 642.63 square kilometers. 45% of population live in Tarnowskie Góry. 21,5% of population are kids and teens. In the county Erlangen-Höchstadt and the county Tarnowskie Góry take place students exchange.
